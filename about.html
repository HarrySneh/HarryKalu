About Chef Kalu/h2>

With over 15 years of culinary experience across Europe's finest restaurants, Chef Kalu brings passion and creativity to every dish. Her journey began in her grandmother's kitchen in Tuscany, where she learned the art of transforming simple ingredients into extraordinary meals.

After training at Le Cordon Bleu in Paris and working at Michelin-starred establishments, Kalu has developed a unique approach that combines classical techniques with modern innovation.

Now exclusively serving weekend culinary experiences, Chef Kalu focuses on creating memorable dining moments for families and special events. Her flexible approach ensures each menu is tailored to your preferences and dietary needs.
